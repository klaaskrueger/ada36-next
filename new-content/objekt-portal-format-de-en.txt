UNIQUE LOFT APARTMENT
Barrier-free and Commission-free!

This charming and spacious 2-room loft offers you an ideal blend of modern interior architecture and contemporary functionality in one of Berlin's most sought-after locations.

Living and working: You can combine them here.

The loft is located on the 3rd floor of a modern residential building on Adalbertstraße.
Still in trendy Mitte and only a few hundred meters to hip Kreuzberg.


LIVING SPACES

The heart of the loft, the main room, includes a fully equipped American kitchen with kitchen island.
There is plenty of space for a generous dining area as well as a sofa landscape for relaxing.

The entire floor area is equipped with high-quality oak parquet flooring.

From the main room you can access the pretty balcony with wooden planks, the bathroom and the second room that can be used as a bedroom.

From the bedroom with enough space for a double bed and a large wall closet, you go directly into the walking closet or storage room. Here you will also find a safe for your valuables.

By opening the wide French doors that reach to the ceiling, you can connect both rooms into one room.

Through the integration of the large window front to the balcony, plenty of natural light also enters the rooms.


GARDEN & OUTDOOR AREA

The garden of the house is a small relaxation oasis and invites you to use it as well.


The loft currently has no tenants and is therefore free for your immediate occupancy.

In recent years it has been successfully rented furnished.
